Connection Instructions:

  1. Connect your ESP8266 to your computer via USB cable
  2. Click "Connect to ESP8266" and select the serial port
  3. Put ESP8266 into flash mode (GPIO0 to GND, then reset)
  4. Load your firmware binary (from URL or file)
  5. Click "Flash Firmware" to start uploading
